About Shine & Gloss Detailing

At Shine & Gloss Detailing, we are passionate about cars and dedicated to providing the highest quality detailing services. Our team of skilled professionals has over 10 years of experience in the car detailing industry, ensuring that your vehicle gets the care it deserves.

Our Story

Shine & Gloss Detailing was founded in 2014 by car enthusiasts who wanted to bring their passion for vehicle care to the local community. Over the years, we have grown from a small operation to one of the leading detailing services in the region, known for our attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced team with over a decade of detailing expertise
  • Use of high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning products
  • Flexible scheduling and mobile services
  • Competitive pricing with excellent results
  • Personalized service tailored to your vehicle’s needs

Our Team

Our team is made up of car lovers who take pride in every detail. We are fully trained in the latest detailing techniques and committed to providing top-notch service. Whether it's a simple wash or a full detailing package, we treat every car like it’s our own.
